Weekly Courses

Creator Workshops is currently offering workshops as an extracurricular program in two locations, Honeydew and Featherbrooke. The Mechanics class is geared toward beginners, normally from Grades 1 - 5. The Robotics class is primarily for more advanced builders, normally from Grade 6 and up. For more information on the material taught in these classes, please see the “What We Teach” section of the home page. If you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to phone or email us.

The Robotics course is geared to prepare more experienced students for the World Robotics Olympiad. In order to participate in this class, the students will need to have their own robotics set. These are the Educational EV3 sets (R 7200), the Retail EV3 sets, or the NXT sets (No longer in production). The Educational EV3 sets can be purchased through Creator Workshops or Hands on Tech. The robotics students will be coached by Evan Cantrell, who has won the SA National branch of the WRO twice, and competed in the Internationals in Indonesia in 2013. Students will compete in teams of either 2 or 3, and need only purchase one robot per team. All other materials necessary for the competition will be provided by Creator Workshops. For more information feel free to contact us.

PRICING

The Featherbrooke class is R 1350 per term for the 90 minute workshops. The Honeydew class is R 950 per term for the 60 minute workshops. In addition to the termly fee, each student must purchase a workbook, which will be used for two terms. The cost of a workbook is R 200.00.
