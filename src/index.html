CREATOR WORKSHOPS

Creativity under the Creator


Creator Workshops is a small business founded in 2017 focused on personal interaction with our students. We use LEGO® Education materials to provide learners with a fundamental knowledge of the principles of engineering and technology in a way that is both instructive and entertaining. Everything we teach students is based on a Biblical worldview of science and technology, which we pass on to students through teaching, discussion, debate and application.
