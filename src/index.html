CREATOR WORKSHOPS

Creativity under the Creator


Creator Workshops uses LEGO Education™ material to give students a dynamic, practical understanding of STEAM concepts. Our goal is to assist students in developing their enjoyment and comprehension of the fundamentals of engineering and problem-solving. Students have a fantastic opportunity to express their creativity as they apply their knowledge of concepts to LEGO models. Creator Workshops also sends select students to the annual World Robotics Olympiad where their capacities are stretched and matured in a competitive environment. We have something to offer to all ages!



What We Offer

Weekly Classes

Classes are offered weekly as an extramural programme at a school or venue in your area. We offer engaging Junior Mechanics (Ages 4 - 7), Mechanics (Ages 8 - 16), and Robotics (Ages 13 - 18) classes. Our weekly classes incorporate innovative material developed by Hands-on-Tech and are CAPS-applicable. Contact us now for a free trial lesson!


More Info

School Workshops

We offer one-day STEAM workshops at schools throughout the school year. These workshops are tailor-made to practically apply concepts students have been learning during the term to exciting LEGO engineering projects. We work with the teachers of each school to ensure that the content of the courses are relevant. Each one-day workshop is divided into individual classes where groups of students attend a presentation which pairs fundamental theory with fascinating LEGO models. Contact us today for a personalised quote!


Contact Us

Holiday Workshops

Every summer and winter, we run multiple holiday workshops in different locations with unique themes. Students will be able to attend LEGO engineering workshops for 1-2 hours a day for a week. Course content is focused on providing stimulating information, friendly competition, and enjoyable application of engineering concepts.
