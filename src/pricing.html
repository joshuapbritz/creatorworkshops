For school workshops we develop a quote based on the number of students attending each class. Please contact us if you are interested.

Special Offers

Trial Session

The first lesson you attend is a free trial session. You will only be charged for subsequent lessons.

Hosting Discount

One child from the hosting family will receive free classes for that term per every five paying students who attend the class that term. Contact us to arrange a class. Subject to scheduling and location.

Family Discount

For families with multiple children enrolled, only the first child pays full price. The second child receives a 10% discount, and any other children receive a 25% discount.

Prepayment Discount

Lessons are ordinarily billed in advance on a monthly basis.If you pay in advance for 15 lessons, you will receive a 10% discount.
