Who We Are

Creator Workshops is a small business founded in 2017 focused on personal interaction with our students. We use LEGO® Education materials to provide learners with a fundamental knowledge of the principles of engineering and technology in a way that is both instructive and entertaining. Everything we teach students is based on a Biblical worldview of science and technology, which we pass on to students through teaching, discussion, debate and application.

Our founder and primary teacher is Timothy Evan Cantrell. He began by taking LEGO Engineering and Robotics classes as a student throughout his schooling. During this time his team won first place in the National Robotics Olympiad twice and represented South Africa at the World Robot Olympiad in Indonesia. His time as a student inspired him to continue in the LEGO Education world as a teacher. He taught for another company for several years before founding Creator Workshops.

Evan is a member at Antioch Bible Church where he has served in various ministries. Because of his personal faith, Evan does not want to simply use students as a source of income, but wants to rather pass on the valuable truths and skills he has learned to the next generation. He has a passion for South Africa's future as a nation and under God. Because of his love for South Africa, Evan declined a number of academic opportunities in the USA. Evan has a BCom Law degree from Wits and is currently in the final year of his LLB. He has signed at Clyde & Co for Practical Vocational Training as a lawyer starting in 2023. Evan is happily married, and lives in Bryanston with his wife Tarryn, who is expecting their first child.
